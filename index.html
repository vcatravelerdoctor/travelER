Program Overview

The VCA Travel ER Program connects skilled emergency veterinarians with hospitals in need of relief support

As a VCA Travel ER veterinarian, you'll have the opportunity to work in various emergency and specialty hospitals across the country. This program provides competitive compensation, flexible scheduling, and comprehensive travel accommodations while helping hospitals in need of relief support.

Licensure Reimbursement

Expand your practice across multiple states

Standard Licensure Coverage

As part of the Travel ER Program, VCA will reimburse associates for:

  • DEA license fees
  • License fees for two states of your choice
  • This is in addition to your regular CE allotment

High-Need Hospital Opportunity

For ER/Specialty hospitals with urgent staffing needs:

  • The hospital will cover state license reimbursement in addition to the two licenses already provided
  • Doctors must apply for licensing within 7 days of accepting the offer
  • Doctors commit to work 12 shifts over 2-3 periods once their license is active
  • This agreement can be utilized multiple times with different high-need hospitals
  • Allows doctors to accrue multiple state licenses if interested in expanding practice reach

Travel Agreement

How we support your travel needs

Distance-Based Reimbursement

🚗

Local Travel (Under 4 hours)

If hospital is within 4 hours drive of associate's home, mileage will be reimbursed

✈️

Long-Distance Travel (Over 4 hours)

If hospital is over a 4-hour drive from associate's home, a flight may be expensed

Accommodation Coverage

🌄

Pre-Shift Accommodation

If shift begins before 2pm, a hotel the night prior to the shift can be expensed

🌃

Post-Shift Accommodation

If shift ends after 2pm, a hotel the night of the shift can be expensed

Additional Travel Guidelines

🚙

Rental Car Coverage

If associate is flying or taking a train, a rental car may be expensed with prior approval

📅

Shift Requirements

When flying to hospital locations, 3 shifts in a row must be worked

Travel Expenses & Rewards

Booking Process: DVMs use AMEX for all charges and book their own travel through our dedicated system.

Travel Rewards: You keep all points earned from travel bookings (e.g., Marriott Bonvoy, airline miles).

Onboarding Process

Getting you ready for success

Training

  • 2 days of comprehensive WOOFWare system training
  • Learn all the essential functions needed for your ER shifts
  • Dedicated time reviewing all Travel ER topics
  • Laurice is your point person for all questions during onboarding

Shadow Program

For quality assurance and your comfort, we require a shadow day at each new hospital location:

  • Shadow shift required for first visit to any new location
  • Shadow day counts toward your shift requirements
  • Even experienced Travel program DVMs need a shadow day at new hospitals

This ensures you're familiar with the hospital's workflow, team members, and systems before starting independent shifts.
