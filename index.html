VCA Travel ER Program

Join our team of dedicated emergency relief veterinarians and make a difference in hospitals across the country with competitive compensation and flexible scheduling.

Program Overview

Emergency relief veterinary services across VCA hospital locations

The VCA Travel ER Program connects skilled emergency veterinarians with hospitals in need of relief support. Work in various emergency and specialty hospitals across the country while enjoying competitive compensation, flexible scheduling, and comprehensive travel accommodations.

Salary-Only Structure

This compensation is salary only with no production bonuses. As a Travel ER veterinarian, you won't have to worry about hospital volume or case load at facilities where you're filling shifts. We provide a generous base salary so you can focus solely on providing excellent patient care without concerns about production metrics.

Licensure Reimbursement

Expand your practice across multiple states

Standard Coverage

As part of the Travel ER Program, VCA will reimburse associates for:

  • DEA license fees
  • License fees for two states of your choice
  • This is in addition to your regular CE allotment

High-Need Hospital Opportunity

For hospitals with urgent staffing needs:

  • Hospital covers additional state license reimbursement beyond the standard two licenses
  • Apply for licensing within 7 days of accepting the offer
  • Commit to 12 shifts over 2-3 periods once license is active
  • Can be utilized multiple times with different high-need hospitals

Travel Agreement

Comprehensive travel reimbursement policies

Travel Reimbursement

  • Local Travel: Mileage reimbursed for hospitals within 4 hours drive
  • Long-Distance: Flights expensed for hospitals over 4 hours away
  • Rental Cars: Available with prior approval when flying/taking train
  • Consecutive Shifts: 3 shifts in a row required when flying

Accommodations

  • Pre-Shift Hotel: Covered if shift begins before 2pm
  • Post-Shift Hotel: Covered if shift ends after 2pm
  • Expenses: Use provided corporate AMEX for all charges
  • Rewards: Keep all travel points earned (hotel, airline, etc.)
